Overview

Purpose

To convince people to make reservations with Whitewater Rafting. The big thing that will have to be promotions that encourage first time customers, to help generate revenue.

Audience

Mostly people who are seeking outdoor recreation and fun. Maybe more adventurous individuals. They are people who have the means due to the corporate livestyle. Are they looking for excitement to enhance their life? Then rafting is the answer. Why do they feel so stagnant in their lives? It's because they aren't learning skills that will make them independent. As they roar down these rapids with us, they will find greater inner peace and satisfaction in their lives.
