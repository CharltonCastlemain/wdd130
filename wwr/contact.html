Meet Our Team

Captain Jack Sparrow - River Guide
Don't let his appearance fool you -- the Captain is more than sober enough to help you embark on an epic journey! This charming swashbuckler knows every single waterway we tour, and you'll have the most hilarious time with his antics!

Sally Jenkins - Trip Coordinator
Our darling Sally will be there to answer all of your questions and concerns. Lovingly referred to the glue of our family, Sally is here to make sure events run smoothly and on schedule.

Chris Stephens - Safety Officer
Don't let those eyes fool you; Chris has a hawks vision for the safety of our courses. Often a trailblazer before the main party, Chris scouts ahead to make sure you and your family have nothing to fear, except for having too much fun!
