Home page

Here at Dark Blue, we understand the real issues of today’s fighter pilots: No matter how much one may train and practice, nothing beats actual experience fighting the enemy. However, in most aviation exercises, pilots of a nation end up practicing against other aircraft of their respective country.
Dark Blue understood this issue and created the first ever private fighter weapons school in history. Now, each and every government can have the opportunity to practice fighting against their enemy, as if it were an actual experience.
Whether you fly aircraft from Boeing, Sukhoi or Dassault, we have the product for you. We offer top-rated combat experience from our pilots, veterans from many different sovereign nations. Send your top pilots to our facility in the high mountains of Idaho for an immersive, and complete, dogfighting encounters. Your pilots may get more flight combat hours here than they ever may fly in their careers.
At the end of our school’s 10 week program, not only do we rank the members on their flying ability, but they will also be able to return to your fleets, training and aiding their comrades in arms. Hopefully, with this course augmenting your military training, you may be able to save lives.

NATO Lineup

Dassault Rafale:
This French aircraft has demonstrated time and time again its superior maneuvering qualities in air-to-air combat, making it a favorite to purchase among nations. With forward canards allowing for superior low speed turning, along with a powerful twin engine and delta wing combination, this aircraft will test your pilots to their limits. Come test your mettle against one of our favorite craft!
Boeing F-15 Eagle:
Known for having an undefeated streak in over 100 engagements, the F-15 may be the most successful fighter aircraft in history. With one of the most incredible power-to-weight ratios around, your pilots may yet be humbled when you fly against this monster of a airframe. With two Pratt & Whitney powerhouses pushing this aircraft to over twice the speed of sound, any experience your pilots gain while fighting our premier pilots in this craft may be the best they get out of their entire time here at Dark Blue.

Russia/China Selection

We understand that the other side of the “iron curtain” may be intriguing for some of our clientele, so we’ve brought out two of our favorites: One from each of the superpowers running in the great expanse of Asia:
Chengdu J-20
Called the “Mighty Dragon” in it’s own tongue, the J-20 is the Chinese military’s premier stealth fighter. Although it’s origins may be controversial, don’t let that dissuade your pilots from learning from its aggressive fighting style. Not only will this give your pilots the ability to practice combat against a stealth aircraft, but the incredible maneuverability that its forward canard wings offer will put even the most veteran of pilots in the backs of their seats.
Mikoyan MiG-35 Fulcrum-F
Our dark horse of the fleet, the MiG-35 is a drastic improvement over its predecessor, the MiG-29, and to this day has never seen actual combat. Don’t let this deter you however, to the incredible capabilities that this aircraft boasts. With a highly updated fly-by-wire system, state of the art avionics and advanced targeting computers may mean your enemy might just be over the horizon, gaining a lock on you. Being able to best this aircraft may be one of the highlights of your pilots career, and give your side quite the edge when comparing your strength to your adversaries.
